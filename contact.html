Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free, no-obligation consultations both in-home and at our Pennsauken showroom. There's no pressure‚Äîjust honest advice tailored to your needs and budget.

Do you use subcontractors for installation?

Never. All installations are completed by our certified in-house team. This ensures quality control, accountability, and peace of mind from start to finish.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve South Jersey, including Pennsauken, Merchantville, Cherry Hill, Camden, and surrounding communities in the Greater Philadelphia area. Call us to confirm availability in your area.

How long does installation take?

It depends on the project scope. Most window and door installations are completed in 1-3 days. Window treatments and awnings can often be installed in a single day. We'll provide a clear timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We work with financing partners to help make your project affordable. Ask about our financing options during your consultation.
