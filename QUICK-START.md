## How Editing Works

### Example: Update Business Hours

1. Log in to `yoursite.com/admin`
2. Click **"Site Settings"**
3. Click **"General Settings"**
4. Find the **"Business Hours"** section
5. Change Monday-Friday to whatever you want
6. Click **"Save"**
7. **Done!** Hours update across the entire site instantly

### Example: Add a New Review

1. Log in to admin
2. Click **"Customer Reviews"**
3. Click **"New Review"**
4. Fill in the form:
   - Customer name
   - Review text
   - Rating (1-5 stars)
   - Source (Google, Angi, etc.)
5. Click **"Publish"**
6. **Done!** Shows up on homepage

### Example: Change Homepage Headline

1. Log in to admin
2. Click **"Home Page"**
3. Click **"Home Page Content"**
4. Edit the **"Headline"** field
5. Click **"Save"**
6. Refresh your site - new headline appears!
